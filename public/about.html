Hi!, I'm Ryan.

My roots are deeply planted in Delaware County, Pennsylvania, just a stone's throw from the vibrant city of Philadelphia.
I am a huge Philadelphia sports fan and grew up playing ice hockey in the surrounding areas.
My academic journey led me to Temple University, where I delved into the world of Risk Management and Insurance, a field that intrigued me with its complexity and dynamism.
After graduating, I embarked on a career as an underwriter, specializing in the niche of private country clubs and luxurious hotels/resorts.

This experience was a gateway to the brokerage side of insurance, where I found my niche representing clients with extensive commercial property portfolios.
It was here, amidst the intricate details of property and risk, that my fascination with real estate began to take shape,
ultimately culminating in the founding of Top Shelf Investment Group.

Outside the office, you'll find me embracing the serenity of nature through boating and fishing, or on the golf course, chasing the elusive perfect swing.
I'm excited about the opportunity to connect with you and explore how we can achieve great things together in the world of real estate!
